Love words around Africa

How to say the word love in different African langauges

In most popular languages in Africa, the word love can be altered in

  • lief, liefhe or liehde: Afrikaans
  • soyayya, kauna or so: Hausa
  • ife or nife: Yoruba
  • ihunaanya or ifunaanya: Igbo

with their indigeneous flavours!
